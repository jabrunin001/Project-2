Netflix: A Story of the Revolutionary Company Netflix's rise to power and what the future holds

Brief History

Netflix was founded in 1997 in Scotts Valley, California. It started as innovate approach to DVD distribution through mail, competing with entertainment giants such as Blockbuster. They are now the largest streaming service with over 200 million subscribers in 190 countries around the world. Their collaboration and growth with international content may be the reason why the giant stays on the rise. This app had the intention of looking at the history of Netlflix (and hopefully the future) to see how it has changed over the years. Looking at key dates in it's history and how they were catalysts for the growth of the company. How the expansion into foreign countries and new original content has spured a new creative enterprise. Finally how the growth of adult centered content and the lack of cable monitoring has allowed more age specific content for everyone to enjoy.
